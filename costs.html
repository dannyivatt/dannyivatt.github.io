Alit.io is a radically cheaper support channel for SaaS providers.

Supporting Users is Expensive

Traditionally, organisations rely on expensive telephone and email or CRM support that still needs a body of support staff. Expert users have no incentive to interact on community forums. How-tos, help manuals and guides are centrally produced and distributed at great expense, despite this knowedge existing amongst expert users.

The cost to the user of dedicated support means that many users are unwilling or unable to pay, whereas low margin products and startups are often unable to provide dedicated user support due to the cost.

The average profit margin for SaaS companies is only 1.3%. Reductions in the cost of providing support services can mean the difference between a profit and loss, especially for new or niche software providers.1
