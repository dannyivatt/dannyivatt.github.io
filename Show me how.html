How does it work?

Alit.io is a user support forum where a community can ask questions, post responses and vote on submissions.

The difference is the reward mechanism we use to incentivize your users to help each other.


A new user submits his query to Alitio asking the community for advice.

Other users recognise the issue and vote on his submission.




An experienced user understands how to solve the issue, and posts his fix. He can also post how tos, documentation, and help other other less experienced users with their ongoing support needs.

Other users vote on the solution, rewarding the experienced user for his contribution.


Over time, users build up a number of points for their contributions to the community. They are rewarded for how they have helped other members.

Each user has an all time points total demonstrating their ongoing contribution to the community and are especially rewarded for providing solutions to popular questions. They also have a redeemable points total. These are the points they have earned since the last time they chose to buy a reward.


A user can redeem their points and browse and choose an item from the store, their reward for contributing to the community.

These items are things supplied by the community owner.

A typical reward would include a month's worth of free licensing, a valuable incentive for providing support to the user community.


As community owner, you have complete control over moderation, rewards and the goals of the forum. You can set goals, award star users, launch competitions and tailor incentives to match your product and support targets.

You can provide active moderation, track costs, and set limits based on your budget.



Advantages

By rewarding the user community you ensure that their knowledge is shared. They are incentivized to help each other and work towards your goals.

An engaged and active community develops and expert help and support can be provided at a low cost to you but with rewards of a very high value to them.

Security

Alit.io is a closed community tied to licensing for your product. Community rules encourage active participation and users can downvote spam and posts that do not contribute to the goals you set. Active moderation ensures that you have complete control over the forum, redemption of points and the store.

Other security measures such as vote fuzzing and normalisation and IP restriction mean that bots, spam and astroturfing are disallowed. These measures ensure that community contributions are on topic and relate to the goals set by you.

Customization and Rewards

Provide rewards that match the goals of your community and the product being suported.

Integrate with Alit.io to offer reduced licensing costs, or free product usage for star users. Offer purchases from your marketplace, or offers from partner organisations.
