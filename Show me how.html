How does it work?

Alit.io is a user support forum where a community can ask questions, post responses and vote on submissions. The difference is the reward mechanism.









A new user submits his query to Alitio asking the community for advice. Other users recognise the issue and vote on his submission.









An experienced user understands how to solve the issue, and posts his fix. He can also post how tos, documentation, and help other other less experienced users with their ongoing support needs. Other users vote on the solution, rewarding the experienced user for his contribution.









Over time, users build up a number of points for their contributions to the community. They are rewarded for how they have helped other members. Each user has an all time points total demonstrating their ongoing contribution to the community. They also have a redeemable points total. These are the points they have earned since the last time he they to buy a reward.









A user can redeem these points and browse and choose an item the store, a reward for their contribution to the community. These items are things chosen by the community owner. Licensing, app store downloads, or other things of relevance to the product being supported.









As community owner, you have complete control over moderation, rewards and the goals of the forum. You can set goals, award star users, launch competitions and tailor incentives to match your product and support targets. Track costs, and set limits based on your budget.









Advantages

By rewarding the user community you ensure that their knowledge is shared. They are incentivized to help each other and work towards your goals, whether supporting your product, or stimulating development on your platform.

By rewarding your users, an engaged and active community develops and expert help and support can be provided at a low cost to you but with a very high value to your users.

Security

Through vote fuzzing and normalisation, IP restriction and active moderation, our security measures mean that bots, spam, fraud, multiple accounts and astroturfing are disallowed. Our measures ensure that community contributions are on topic and relate to the goals set for your community.

Optionally create a closed, invite only community tied to licensing for your product.

Customisation and Rewards

Provide rewards that match the goals of your community and the product being suported. Integrate with Alit.io to offer reduced licensing costs, or free product usage for star users. Offer app store downloads or free development tools to incentivize your developer community.
