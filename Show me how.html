How does it work?

Evangalos is a new user. He submits his query to alitio asking the community for advice. Other users recognise the issue and vote on his submission.

Stevie is an experienced developer. He understands how to solve Evangalos’s issue, and posts a reply.

Evangalos tries Stevie’s fix. It works.

Evangalos and other users vote on Stevie’s solution, rewarding him for his contribution.

Over time, Stevie has built up a number of points for his contributions to the community. He has posted how tos, documentation, and helped other other less experienced users with their questions.

Stevie views his profile page. he has an all time points total demonstrating his ongoing contribution to the community. He also has a redeemable points total. these are the points he has earned since the last time he chose to buy a reward.

Stevie views his store. He has 762 points to spend. He browses and chooses a month’s worth of free licensing as a reward for his contribution to the community.

As community owner, you have complete control over moderation, rewards and the goals of the forum. You can set goals, award star users, launch competitions and tailor incentives to match your product and support targets. Track costs, and set limits based on your budget.

Advantages

By rewarding the user community to work to ensure that their knowledge is shared. They are incentivized to help each other and work towards your goals whether supporting your product, or stimulated development of your platform.

By rewarding your users, an engaged and active community develops and expert help and support can be provided at a low cost to you but with a very high value to the user.

Security

Through vote fuzzing and normalisation, IP restriction and active moderation, our security measures mean that bots, spam, fraud, multiple accounts and astroturfing are disallowed. Our measures ensure that community contributions are on topic and relate to the goals set for your community.

Optionally create a closed, invite only community tied to licensing for your product.

Customisation and Rewards

Alit.io is ideally suited to help technical communities support each other. Experienced developers are rewarded, while less experienced developers learn, ask questions, and grow while working to produce applications for your platform.

Rewards that match the goals of your community and the product being suported can be provided. Integrate your with alitio to offerreduced licensing costs, or free product usage for star users. Offer app store downloads and free development tools to incentivize your developer community.

Costs

By offering rewards related to your product, you give users incentives of real value, but at a reduced expense to you. You set the limits and conversion rate for user’s points totals and only reward the contributions you and your community find valuable.
