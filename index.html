Alit.io is an incentivized community support platform.


Crowdsource expert knowledge from within your user community.

Reward development on your platform.

Radically reduce the cost of providing expert user support.

Software companies are faced with the challenge of reducing costs, while still meeting user support needs.

Expert knowledge exists within user communities, yet companies still rely on expensive telephone and email support, inactive user forums and uncontrolled peer-to-peer knowedge sharing.

Alit.io helps you own and manage interaction with your user community, incentivize them to help each other, create an expert knowledge base and stimulate development on your product or platform.

You provide rewards to the community based on how their peers rate and rank their contributions. The community are incentivized to develop for your platform, support each other to use your product or contribute to your ecosystem.

For organisations

Alit.io is a radically cheaper alternative to traditional support channels. Incentivize your user community to support each other by offering tangible incentives and rewards that you control. By creating an Alit.io community, you can replace expensive customer support channels with the expert knowledge that exists amongst your users.

For growing platforms

Alit.io provides a way to incentivise development on your new platform and stimulate a thriving app ecosystem. The developer community is rewarded for creating new apps, supporting each other, sharing code and expertise, producing how-tos, APIs and documentation and evangelising to their peers.

For developer communities

Alit.io is ideally suited to reward technical communities for supporting each other. Experienced developers are rewarded, while less experienced developers learn, ask questions, and grow while working to produce applications for your platform.

For users

Inexperienced users ask questions, tap into an expert, community created knowledge base and vote for the submissions that contribute most to their learning. They can grow, share and get effective support.

Experienced users respond to questions, provide how-tos and documentation, contribute to the knowledge base and give expert advice. Users vote on each other's submissions. They can redeem those votes for items of real value that you provide.

How does it work?

See how Alit.io can radically save on the time and money required to provide user support and create an expert knowledge base through rewarding your users or how you can incentivize a strong evangelising community of developers for your platform.
