Alit.io is an incentivized community support platform for SaaS providers.


Crowdsource expert knowledge from within your user community.

Incentivize users with licensing and rewards that you own and control.

Radically reduce the cost of providing expert user support.

Create customer success by incentivizing your users to help each other.

SaaS companies are faced with the challenge of meeting user support needs within tight margins.

Expert knowledge exists within user communities, yet SaaS companies rely on expensive telephone and email support, inactive user forums and uncontrolled peer-to-peer knowedge sharing.

Alit.io helps you own and manage interaction with your user community by incentivizing them to help each other and create an expert knowledge base.

With Alit.io, a user's karma, points or kudos gained from forum interactions has a real value tied to your product licensing, market place or app store.

They can redeem their forum points and get rewarded for helping new users, providing ongoing support, or writing guides and documentation.

For SaaS organisations

Alit.io is a radically cheaper alternative to traditional support channels. Incentivize your user community to support each other by offering tangible incentives and rewards that you control. By creating an Alit.io community, you can supplement or replace expensive customer support channels with the expert knowledge that exists amongst your users.

For growing products

Providing support to users when you are challenged with growing a new product or platform is difficult and expensive. We provide a platform that allows you to reward your users for supporting each other, with incentives that you own and control.

For technical communities

Alit.io is ideally suited to reward technical communities for supporting each other. Experienced users are rewarded, while less experienced users learn, ask questions, and grow while working to customise your product or to produce applications for your ecosystem.

For users

Inexperienced users ask questions, tap into an expert, community created knowledge base and vote for the submissions that contribute most to their learning. They can grow, share and get effective support.

Experienced users respond to questions, provide how-tos and documentation, contribute to the knowledge base and give expert advice. Users vote on each other's submissions. They can redeem those votes for items of real value that you provide.

How does it work?

Alit.io is aimed specifically at Software as a Service companies. See how you can crowdsource an expert knowledge base, provide highly effective user support and incentivize customer success.
